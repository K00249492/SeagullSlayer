Seagull Slayer



Description

Seagull Slayer is a simple arcade-style game, created in Unity. Play as a robotic crow on a mission to take down some very mean-looking seagulls and pelicans.


Gameplay

The arrow keys are used to move and the space bar is used to fire.

Watch out - the more seagulls and pelicans you defeat, the faster more will come!

Use the powerups you find to aid you in your battle and maximise your final score.

Good Luck!
